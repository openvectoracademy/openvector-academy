Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about OpenVector Academy

How do I enroll in a course? +

You can enroll in any course by visiting our Courses page, selecting the course you're interested in, and clicking the "Enroll Now" button. You'll need to create an account or log in to complete the enrollment process.

What are the course fees? +

Course fees vary depending on the program. We offer both free introductory courses and paid comprehensive programs. You can view the exact pricing on each course page. We also offer financial aid and scholarships for eligible students.

Do you offer certificates upon completion? +

Yes! All our paid courses come with a certificate of completion. These certificates are recognized by industry partners and can be shared on LinkedIn and other professional networks.

What is your refund policy? +

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all our paid courses. If you're not satisfied with the course content or delivery, you can request a full refund within 30 days of enrollment.

Do you offer technical support? +

Yes, we provide technical support for all platform-related issues. You can reach our technical support team via email at techsupport@openvector.edu or through the contact form above. Our team is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST.
