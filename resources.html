Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our resources

How do I access the resources?

All resources are available for immediate download after clicking the download button. Some premium resources may require registration or enrollment in specific courses.

Are the resources free?

Most resources are free for registered students. Some advanced or premium materials may require course enrollment. All resources marked with the download button are available at no additional cost.

Can I request specific resources?

Yes! We welcome resource requests. Please use the contact form or email resources@openvector.edu with your suggestions. Our team reviews requests weekly and creates new resources based on student demand.

How often are new resources added?

We add new resources weekly. Subscribe to our newsletter or follow us on social media to get notifications about new materials, updates, and featured collections.

Can I contribute resources?

Absolutely! We accept contributions from students and instructors. Please contact our resource team with your submission. All contributions are reviewed for quality and relevance before being added to the library.
